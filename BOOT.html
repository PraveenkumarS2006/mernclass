KGF: Chapter 2
"KGF: Chapter 2" is the 2022 sequel to the blockbuster hit "KGF: Chapter 1", directed by Prashanth Neel. Starring Yash as Rocky, the film picks up where the first left off, with Rocky now ruling the Kolar Gold Fields. He must now maintain supremacy while facing government opposition and new powerful enemies like Adheera (played by Sanjay Dutt). With intense action, powerful dialogues, and stylized visuals, Chapter 2 elevates the scale and drama of the franchise. The film was praised for Yash's performance, BGM by Ravi Basrur, and larger-than-life storytelling, becoming one of the highest-grossing Indian films ever.
Actors: Yash, Sanjay Dutt, Raveena Tandon, Srinidhi Shetty
Genre: Action, Crime, Drama
IMDb Rating: 8.3/10
Year: 2022
Director: Prashanth Neel
RRR
"RRR" is a 2022 epic action drama directed by S.S. Rajamouli, set in pre-independence India. It tells the fictional story of two revolutionaries, Alluri Sitarama Raju and Komaram Bheem, played by Ram Charan and Jr. NTR, who unite to fight against British colonial rule. The film is known for its breathtaking action sequences, emotional storytelling, and dazzling cinematography. With international acclaim and awards, including an Oscar for Best Original Song ("Naatu Naatu"), RRR became a global sensation and cemented Rajamouli’s place as a master storyteller.
Actors: Ram Charan, N.T. Rama Rao Jr., Alia Bhatt, Ajay Devgn
Genre: Action, Drama, Historical Fiction
IMDb Rating: 7.9/10
Year: 2022
Director: S.S. Rajamouli
JOE
"JOE" is a 2025 Indian coming-of-age drama film that follows the emotional journey of a passionate young teacher named Joe, who returns to his hometown to work at a government school. Faced with challenges from both the system and his personal life, Joe becomes a mentor, friend, and voice of change for his students. The film captures themes of love, sacrifice, and education’s power to transform lives. Set in a beautiful village backdrop, "JOE" is a heartfelt tribute to educators who inspire and fight for a better future.
